APT29 Cozy Bear - Threat Intelligence Report

Report ID: TIR-2024-001

Classification: TLP:AMBER

Date: December 2024

Executive Summary

This report details recent activities attributed to APT29 (also known as Cozy Bear, The Dukes, and IRON HEMLOCK). The threat group is associated with the Russian Foreign Intelligence Service (SVR) and has been active since at least 2008.

Recent campaigns have targeted government organizations using sophisticated malware including Cobalt Strike and custom backdoors. The group exploited vulnerabilities including CVE-2021-44228 (Log4Shell) and CVE-2023-23397.

Related Threat Actors

APT29 has been linked to the GRU (Russian military intelligence) and works in coordination with APT28 (Fancy Bear). The group has also deployed Emotet in some campaigns for initial access.

Tools commonly used include Mimikatz for credential theft and Cobalt Strike for post-exploitation.
